Seoul, South Korea

Seoul, South Korea, is a vibrant tapestry of modernity and tradition, where sleek skyscrapers coexist with ancient palaces and bustling street markets. Immerse yourself in the city's rich cultural heritage by exploring historic landmarks like Gyeongbokgung Palace and Bukchon Hanok Village. Indulge in the dynamic culinary scene, from sizzling street food to Michelin-starred restaurants. Dive into the energy of districts like Gangnam and Itaewon for shopping and nightlife, or find serenity in the scenic beauty of Namsan Park and the Han River. Seoul seamlessly blends the old and the new, offering an unforgettable journey through Korea's past and present.
